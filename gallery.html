Come Heal & Rest

Cozy Cabins Wellness Resort
Lumby | British Columbia | Canada

Cozy Cabins Wellness Resort is a private, family-centric vacation destination located on 40 acres next to Echo Lake, BC. It features its own private lake and is surrounded by stunning wilderness.

With 7 rustic and well-equipped cabins spread throughout the property, this is the ultimate place for families to relax and reconnect. We aim to provide an environment where families can experience healing and rest.

Cozy Cabins is open in summer, spring, fall, and winter. We are often fully booked in the summer, so book early or request to be added to our waiting list. Winter is a magical time, with cabins featuring wood-burning stoves surrounded by snow, offering a unique and calming experience in nature.
